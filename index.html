Distorted Outcome

  • Every campaign shows the same age bell curve peaking at 45-54
  • “Women 25-54” campaigns report ~50/50 male/female
  • Six- and seven-figure financial give-backs
  • Customer Pulse reports withheld from clients
  • Analytics and Data Cloud reports disagree for same campaign
  • Teams rebuild targeting directly in DSP as a workaround

Fixed Outcome

  • Audience sizes match across ZMP, DSP, and reporting
  • Demographics reflect actual targeting — no phantom bell curves
  • Reachability known before launch — no mid-flight surprises
  • Reports compare intent vs. delivery with automated divergence flags
  • All systems use ZID + single demographic source — reports agree
  • Household / cross-device / pacing impacts visible and separated
